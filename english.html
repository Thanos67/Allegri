WHO WE ARE

THE INTERNATIONAL CHOIR “ALLEGRI”, despite its new name, is in essence the continuation of the choir “Allegri” from 2012, has contributed to the musical and cultural events of the city of Kalamata since 2012 and has 26 members.

The choir was constituted a cultural association with the above name in November 2020. It has since welcomed new members and continues to be an international choir. Its members come from many different European countries such as: England, Ukraine, Holland, Moldavia, Russia, Germany, Serbia, Austria, Italy, Switzerland, Estonia and of course Greece. .

The choir members’ excellent cooperation, their love of choral music and the sharing of different cultural backgrounds have enriched the choir and therefore resulted in work of a high standard.

The choir’s musical programme has a diverse repertoire which includes a wide variety of Greek songs and works from around the world ranging from traditional and modern songs, to classical music and religious works as well as world and Greek hits.

OUR GOALS AND DREAMS


We wish to continue to serve choral music by cooperating with other musicians and ensembles and enriching our repertoire. We persevere with added incentive and always have in mind our supportive members both old and new.


Our maestro Mrs Aggeliki Lazarou, continues to guide us on our beautiful musical journeys encouraging and supporting us with patience and perseverance.
